Donate


NSK is a volunteer women lead organization which is dedicated to improve the lives of needy. It needs support from people from all round the globe to support the cause of the organization. You may sponsor a child's schooling, you may second the activities being carried by NSK, you may contribute by buying the souvenir produced by volunteers, women group and communities being supported by NSK.
